El Paso Pavement Conditions Explorer

Please use the slider and layer buttons below to adjust the information displayed on the map to the right. This tool serves to assist the Capital Improvements Department in prioritizing road repair projects by overlaying pavement conditions scores on different layers of interest.

Filter by PCI Score

Pavement Conditions Index (PCI) scores are based off of factors such as presence of potholes, bumps, or cracks. The index ranges from a qualitative scale of Failing to Good or quantitatively 1 to 100. Typically there is a significant drop in condition of a road after a certain amount of time.
